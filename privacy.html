Privacy Policy

App Name: P5 Synth Piano MIDI
Publisher: Sinther
Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Overview

P5 Synth Piano MIDI is a synthesizer and MIDI piano roll application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we do and do not collect.

Data Collection

P5 Synth Piano MIDI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal details to use.

MIDI Data

The app reads MIDI input from connected devices solely to enable real-time music playback and recording within the app. This data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

Internet Access

The app does not connect to the internet or any external servers during normal use.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
